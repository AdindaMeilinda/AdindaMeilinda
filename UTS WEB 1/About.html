About Us

Janji Jiwa Coffee is dedicated to bringing you the finest coffee experience. Founded in 2007 by two coffee enthusiasts, Edward Tirtanata and James Prananto, Janji Jiwa has been at the forefront of the local coffee industry in Indonesia.

We take pride in using only the best local coffee beans sourced from Indonesia. Our commitment to quality extends from the careful selection of coffee beans to meticulous processing.
